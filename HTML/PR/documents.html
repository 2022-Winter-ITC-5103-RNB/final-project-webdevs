  1. English / French Language Test

    2. - English & French are the 2 official languages of Canada. Applicant need to prove that he is proficient in reading, writing, listening & speaking in any one of languages from French, English You can appear for both the tests to score more CRS points.

    - There are two testing systems are available for French and IELTS language. IELTS and CELPIP are english assessment system while TEF and TCF are french assessment system. Result of both language has two years of validity and cost of exam is approximately CAD $250. The better you score in your language test, the higher your overall crs score.

  2. CRS score Calculation

    3. - Crs score depends on study, marital status, age, work experience, ielts score,etc. Click here to calculate CRS score.
    Note:- You will receive an ITA(Invitation to apply ) letter if crs score meets the current express entry\ draw cut-off that are held every 15 days.

  3. ECA (Education Credential Assessment) Report

    4. - ECA is a document certifying and verifying that his education is equivalent to canadian education standards. Applicant can get his ECA done from evaluation agencies like CES, IQAS,ICAS, ICES and WES. WES is the most popular and fastest evaluation agency for getting ECA. Validity of ECA is 5 years and procesing time to get ECA is aroung 1 month to 4 months in different agencies.

    8. Work experience letter and reference letter is required for each job declared in the application ,and these letters should be in CIC format.
